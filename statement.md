## Problem Statement 
People often forget to drink enough water throughout the day. Lack of 
hydration affects concentration, mood, and health. This project aims 
to solve the problem by calculating personalized water requirements 
and generating periodic reminders. 

---

## Scope of the Project
The Hydration Coach project focuses on helping users monitor and improve their daily water intake.
It covers calculating personalized hydration needs, tracking consumption, and providing timely drinking reminders. 
The system also allows users to view their progress and maintain consistent hydration habits. 
Its scope is limited to basic health guidance and does not provide medical advice.

---

## Target Users
The Hydration Coach is designed for individuals who want to monitor and improve their daily water intake. 
It is especially useful for students, working professionals, fitness enthusiasts, and people with busy routines who often forget to stay hydrated.
Beginners learning healthy habits and anyone looking for a simple hydration-tracking tool can also benefit from this project.

---

## High-level features
Daily Water Tracking
Allows users to log their water consumption and monitors progress throughout the day.

Hydration Reminders
Sends timely notifications or prompts reminding users to drink water.

Progress Summary
Displays total water consumed vs. required intake to help users understand their hydration status.

User-Friendly Interaction
Simple, guided input and clear outputs make it easy for anyone to use the program.
